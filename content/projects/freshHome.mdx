Fresh Home is an ecommerce web application built with Next.js, TypeScript,
MongoDB, Prisma, and Tailwind CSS, framer-Motion. The application allows
users to browse products, add products to a shopping cart, checkout their
orders and track their order and deployed to [Vercel](https://vercel.com/).

## Tech stack

Here is the tech stack used in this project:

- NextJS
- Typescript
- Tailwind
- MongoDB
- Prisma
- Vercel

## Features

Here are some of the features:

- Browse products: Users can view a list of products and click on a product
  to view its details.

- Add to cart: Users can add a product to their shopping cart by clicking
  on the "Add to Cart" button.

- View cart: Users can view the contents of their shopping cart, remove
  items from the cart, and adjust the quantity of items.

- Checkout: Users can checkout their order by providing their shipping and
  payment details.

- Authentication: Users can sign up and log in to their account to view
  their order history and save their shipping and payment details for
  future orders.
